VjoHanaba Functions

Smart Tasking

VjoHanaba will translate your natural-language text assignment into components, find keywords, tags, and intentions. Then it will appoint those tasks to the people with the most suitable skill set.

Resource Management

The app seamlessly redistributes workload between all team members. Moreover it will automatically compound task into a bundles to maintain the high pace of work.

Find Potential

The app reveals team members’ strong sides based on their historical performance rate and assigns tasks accordingly.

Teamwork Improvement

VjoHanaba ties people who showed the best results working together and redistributes complex tasks based on teamwork performance index.

Performance Statistics

Assign tasks and promote people based on their contribution to every single task. That way you can encourage the higher input of your employees not overtimes.
